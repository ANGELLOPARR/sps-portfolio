a little about me...

i'm currently a soon-to-be senior at California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo. most people just call it "Cal Poly" for short. my major is computer science and hopefully soon i'll be a full-time software engineer.

sometimes i think i have too many interests in the computer science field, and it often gets hard choose my favorites.

one of my favorite things to do is develop full-stack web applications. initially, i taught myself how to develop frontend applications because i loved the design aspect of it. though i've never been great with a pencil and paper, i found that i could translate my thoughts into designs using code much more effectively. as i advanced in my computer science education, i realized the uses of backend code as well, and soon taught myself that as well.
